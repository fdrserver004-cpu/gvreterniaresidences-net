About Yatharth Eternia

Eternia is inspired by the Anthurium flower, symbolizing timeless beauty, strength, and lasting elegance. Located in Tech Zone IV, Greater Noida West, the project sits on a wide 130-meter road in a prime, fully developed sector. Right in front of a 100-meter wide green belt, Eternia offers peaceful living away from congestion.

Eternia By Yatharth Spread across 6 acres, the community features six impressive G+30 towers designed with a sense of balance and openness. Each tower includes 4 high-speed lifts for easy access, and the smart floor plans ensure less wasted space and more room to live and breathe.
